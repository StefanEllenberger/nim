nim - the game

the rules

the rules of Nim are simple

  • the game starts with n marbles in the center
  • player 1 may select between 1 and x marbles to take away from the center
  • player 2 may then also select between 1 and x marbles to take away from the center
  • the game ends when all marbles are gone from the center
  • the winner is the player who last removed a marble

the players may define n and x at the beginning of the game. x must be at least 2, n must be at least 12.
